swallow

when i say i'm having a hard time no one comes running
because i'm stable and i'll figure it out
i get my work done, exercise and laugh
i don't lash out
i don't stop turning up and tuning in
i can function
and not just function but thrive
i've learned to bandage my own wounds
suture when needed
remedy my own sickness
splint my own broken bones
but there's a poison in me i don't have the antidote to
i hold her in my mouth like a second tongue
swollen and serpent
some days i can swallow her and she lives in the pits of my stomach
bubbling with the acid
my chest burns but i keep breathing
it feels like i don't have a choice
i don't have an oxygen tank
no scuba gear
somehow i can use my lungs to breathe underwater
but for how long
how long until i'm taken into the drink
before my lungs are filled with saltwater
before my alveoli swell and burst
before the poison pours out from my mouth and eyes and consumes me
before i can't get my work done, exercise, and laugh?
we won't know
for now my tongue will stay forked between prison bars
behind a close lipped smile
behind the facade
i'll keep swallowing her
holding her down down down
