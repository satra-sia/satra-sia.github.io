home

this is a home where love does not live
where it does not grow or flourish
but is earned
where it is given and taken away and not shared
this is a home where pride and reward live in love's place
under blue-white light
faux daylight for night work and not conversation
where blinds are turned closed tight for invisibility
sunlight squeezes its way through in dribbles not floods
into warehouse rooms of tools and instruments
with too few chairs for christmas dinner
this is a home where love is performed
quite well too
so at a glance you cannot see through the sham
love is performed but not felt
a son winces at his mother's touch
a daughter eats each meal alone
a mother wonders why her children are silent

this is a home where love is declared at occassions where it should be
and forgotten at most
where the words exist to abolve guilt and repair damage
but have no place otherwise.
this is a home where tears are cried in silence
and smiles are tight-lipped
where photos are forced and never hung on walla lest it feel too homely
where comfort is in things and not each other

this is a home where love does not live
despite convincing appearances.
and despite best efforts it is too late for it to grow
so instead,
son and daughter will have to seek it elsewhere
and mother will end up alone.
