telescope

shut me up like a telescope
put me in your backpack
take me far away from here
look through me at stars i’ll never see
planets i’ll never know
moon’s i’ll never shoot clear
leave me on your bookshelf
a fad and forgotten
find happiness elsewhere
Smudge scratch crack my lens
Let me gather dust
Take me out on cloudy nights
To see your light polluted sky
Put me in your attic
Forget I exist
Let me be a relic you don’t treasure and don’t miss
Shut me up like a telescope
Car boot sell me
Give me away
Let me disappoint someone new
Let me remind them how far the stars are
How like me they’ll never reach them
How saturns rings won’t ever fit
How they’ll never feel Jupiter’s four moonshine
How they’re stuck on this dying rock
With a telescope with a cracked lens
