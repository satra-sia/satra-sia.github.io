You know i could never lie to you
Your ink black pools were truth serums to me
So when i said i would miss you, you know that was in candour
But you’re everywhere
In my mind, my bed, my beaten path
My favourite bridge and little laughs
I catch myself reminiscing
but really you’re sitting in my shadow
Your presence your memories fill my lungs like smog
I’m struggling to breathe these days
My blackened lungs burn as i inhale
You surround me
In the quiet, in the loud
I flinch
I obsess
I hear your 4 rings for me

I told you i would miss you in candour
But the truth changes like the wind

your inky pools black holes swallowing the love i had
Quicker than id like
It hurts to fall out of love with you, too
I’m being as honest as i can
I never wanted to hate you
I wanted to wish you well
I wanted to keep a place for you in my heart
I wanted to love you forever

The fire in the pit of my stomach
The hellscapes you draw on my skin
